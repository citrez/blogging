Machine learning is a large topic, but if you are ever at the cross roads and the choice is between picking an unfamiliar novel technique and an old classic. Be very sure that there is tangable advantage to going with the more complex solution.

There is a takeoff between predictive power and interpretablilty. Sometime i feel like this graph should be stapled to my forehead.
